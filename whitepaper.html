1. INTRODUCTION

Memecoins have showcased the incredible force of community in the crypto landscape. Yet, much of this energy often fuels short-lived hype rather than creating lasting value or real-world utility.

Oblium (OBLM) was created to transform that energy — turning community power into a sustainable, purposeful ecosystem.

Initially launched as a high-utility SPL token on Solana, Oblium leverages Solana's unmatched scalability, sub-second finality, and near-zero transaction fees to deliver an engaging, meme-powered experience.

This white paper presents a clear roadmap for Oblium's evolution — from a community token to a purpose-driven blockchain network built for scalability, transparency, and impact.

2. WHAT IS OBLIUM (OBLM)?

Oblium is a dual-phase project that blends community engagement with advanced blockchain infrastructure.

Phase 1 – The Solana Era

Oblium begins as a meme-powered SPL token on Solana. It powers staking, governance, farming, and meme competitions.

Users earn Impact Points through participation, which convert monthly into OBLM tokens via a verifiable on-chain process.

Phase 2 – The Oblium Chain

Oblium will evolve into a sovereign Layer-1 blockchain built using the Cosmos SDK, secured by Nominated Proof-of-Stake (NPoS).

The Oblium Chain is engineered for AI-assisted smart contracts, verifiable data oracles, and cross-chain interoperability.

Oblium is not just a meme — it's a mission:

Built for dreamers. Fueled by believers. Powered by the people.

3. CORE PURPOSE & MECHANICS

Oblium's foundation lies in transforming participation into measurable value through a transparent reward system:

1. Gamified Points Engine

Users earn non-transferable Impact Points by:

  • Providing liquidity to Oblium-approved pools
  • Participating in governance and proposals
  • Completing quests, educational challenges, or meme creation contests

2. Monthly Conversion Cycle

A smart contract takes a monthly snapshot of users' Impact Points. An algorithm — factoring in total network activity, TVL, and user engagement — determines each participant's OBLM rewards.

This creates a cycle of fairness and sustainability, keeping users actively engaged while aligning rewards with real ecosystem growth.
