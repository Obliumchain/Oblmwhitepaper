1. Decentralization & Security

Consensus: Based on Nominated Proof-of-Stake (NPoS), ensuring validator diversity and community-driven security.

Data Integrity: Uses Zero-Knowledge Proofs (ZKPs) to verify data authenticity without exposing sensitive information.

2. AI Integration

Off-Chain AI Oracles: AI models run off-chain via Chainlink Functions or similar networks, sending cryptographically proven results to Oblium contracts.

Bias Transparency: Each verified AI model logs its dataset hash on-chain, enabling permanent auditability.

Architecture: Built using the Cosmos SDK, achieving 1,000–10,000 TPS through optimized block propagation.

IBC Connectivity: Seamless interoperability with other Cosmos-based networks.

4. Interoperability

Native bridges to Solana and Ethereum ensure liquidity continuity.

Full IBC compatibility enhances cross-chain communication.

8. CORE PURPOSE & MECHANICS

Oblium's foundation lies in transforming participation into measurable value through a transparent reward system:

1. Gamified Points Engine

Users earn non-transferable Impact Points by:

  • Providing liquidity to Oblium-approved pools
  • Participating in governance and proposals
  • Completing quests, educational challenges, or meme creation contests

2. Monthly Conversion Cycle

A smart contract takes a monthly snapshot of users' Impact Points. An algorithm — factoring in total network activity, TVL, and user engagement — determines each participant's OBLM rewards.

This creates a cycle of fairness and sustainability, keeping users actively engaged while aligning rewards with real ecosystem growth.

3. Bonus Rewards & Leaderboards

To make farming more interactive, Oblium introduces bonus rewards for top performers each month.

The most active farmers unlock:

  • Token boosts
  • Exclusive NFTs
  • Special community perks

A global leaderboard showcases top contributors — turning farming into a social and competitive experience, inspiring users to climb higher and stay engaged.

9. FAIR & TRANSPARENT TOKEN CLAIMING

One of the most frustrating experiences in crypto launches is the confusion between TGE (Token Generation Event), claiming, and exchange listing.

Many projects force users to claim minutes before listing, causing site crashes, network congestion, and unfair access delays.

Oblium is different.

We're committed to fairness and transparency — ensuring every holder has an equal start.

That's why Oblium will separate the Claim Date from the Listing Date:

Claim Date (TGE)

Users can claim tokens smoothly, ahead of time.

Listing Date

Exchange trading begins after all participants have had a fair chance to claim.

This guarantees:

  • No rushed last-minute claims
  • Equal opportunity for every holder
  • A smoother, community-friendly launch

Claiming is for the community. Listing is for the market.

14. TRANSITION FRAMEWORK — FROM SOLANA TO OBLIUM CHAIN

Phase 1 — Launch on Solana (Current Stage)

Oblium begins its journey on Solana, leveraging its fast, low-cost, and secure infrastructure to establish early growth and global accessibility.

Key Highlights

Token Type: SPL (Solana Program Library)

Advantages:

  • Lightning-fast transactions and low fees
  • Access to Solana DEXs (Raydium, Jupiter, Orca)
  • Wide wallet compatibility (Phantom, Solflare, etc.)

Purpose:

  • Build a strong, engaged community
  • Enable presale, farming, and DAO participation
  • Begin AI and human-impact integration pilots

Starting on Solana allows Oblium to scale quickly and connect globally while laying the groundwork for its independent blockchain.

Phase 2 — Development of ObliumChain (AI + Human-Centered Blockchain)

ObliumChain represents the next leap — an AI-integrated, human-centered blockchain designed to combine trustless transparency with intelligent computation.

Core Features

  • AI-assisted smart contracts
  • On-chain verified credential systems
  • Secure encrypted data layers for human data
  • AI-optimized Proof-of-Stake consensus for energy efficiency

Technical Goals

  • Launch testnet 12–18 months after token launch
  • Introduce validator incentives and staking systems
  • Bridge with Solana, Ethereum, and BSC for cross-chain liquidity

ObliumChain will evolve into a dedicated blockchain environment where human creativity and AI innovation coexist and thrive.

Phase 3 — Mainnet Launch & Token Migration

Once ObliumChain is live, OBLM transitions from being a Solana-based token to a fully native coin within its own blockchain ecosystem.

Migration Process

  • Snapshot: Record all wallet balances holding $OBLM (SPL)
  • Swap Portal: Deploy a secure, audited bridge allowing 1:1 token swaps from Solana OBLM → Native OBLM
  • Lock & Burn: Swapped Solana tokens are locked or burned to ensure supply integrity
  • Activation: Native OBLM goes live — powering staking, governance, and transaction fees on ObliumChain

This process mirrors the evolution of projects like BNB and Polygon — starting on existing blockchains, then transitioning to their own ecosystems.

Phase 4 — Expansion & Integration

Following migration, ObliumChain expands its ecosystem through partnerships, AI integration, and developer tools.

Post-Migration Objectives

  • Launch decentralized AI marketplaces and oracles
  • Support dApps focused on education, healthcare, and sustainability
  • Establish global partnerships with NGOs and research institutions
  • Introduce developer grants to accelerate innovation

ObliumChain becomes the bridge between blockchain and human progress — where community energy fuels real-world change.
